Acheron is a remarkably powerful DPS that can annihilate enemies with her powerful Ultimate that can ignore weakness type and does not have an energy cost.

If you are looking for a very strong Hypercarry that can also perform well against enemies not weak to Lightning, then Acheron is a must-pull!

Skills

Basic ATK: Trilateral Wiltcross

Deals Lightning DMG equal to 100% of Acheron's ATK to a single target enemy.

Skill: Octobolt Flash

When Acheron uses her Skill on an enemy, she will gain 1 stack of Slashed Dream and apply 1 stack of Crimson Knot to the main target.

This inflicts Lightning DMG equal to 160% of Acheron's ATK on the target, as well as Lightning DMG equal to 60% of her ATK on adjacent enemies.

Ultimate: Slashed Dream Cries in Red

Acheron's Ultimate sequentially unleashes Rainblade 3 times and Stygian Resurge 1 time, dealing up to 372% of her ATK in Lightning DMG to a single target and up to 300% to other enemies.
Rainblade removes up to 3 stacks of Crimson Knot from the target, dealing additional Lightning DMG (15% of ATK per stack removed, up to 60%).

Stygian Resurge deals 120% ATK in Lightning DMG to all enemies and removes all Crimson Knots.

Crimson Knot cannot be applied during the Ultimate.
Acheron's Ultimate reduces enemy Toughness and All-Type RES, and the first three attacks target selected enemies, with the fourth dealing DMG to all enemies.

Technique: Quadrivalent Ascendance

Immediately attacks the enemy.

At the start of each wave, gains Quadrivalent Ascendance, dealing Lightning DMG equal to 200% of Acheron's ATK to all enemies and reducing Toughness of all enemies irrespective of Weakness Types.

When Weakness is broken, triggers the Lightning Weakness Break effect.

Quadrivalent Ascendance: After using the Ultimate, Acheron gains 1 point(s) of Slashed Dream and inflicts 1 stack(s) of Crimson Knot on a single random enemy.

If attacking a normal enemy, immediately defeats them without entering combat.

When not hitting any enemies, no Technique Points are consumed.

Optimal Gear

Along the Passing Shore

Light Cone

Why?:

As it is Acherons Signature Light COne it naturaly becomes the best Light Cone for her, as it increases Acheron's DMG and provides an extra Slashed Dream stack when she uses her Skill.

Effect:

Increases CRIT DMG by 36%. Hitting an enemy inflicts Mirage Fizzle for 1 turn (once per target per attack). Deals 24% more DMG to enemies with Mirage Fizzle, with the Ultimate's DMG increasing by the same amount.

Pioneer Diver of Dead Waters

Relic Set

Why?:

Pioneer Diver of Dead Waters is Acheron's best Relic set as it provides her CRIT Rate and increased CRIT DMG against debuffed enemies.

Izumo Gensei and Takama Divine Realm

Ornament Set

Why?:

Izumo Gensei can increase Acheron's CRIT Rate if there's at least one Nihility unit in the party.

This works extremely well for Acheron as her best teams consist of other Nihility units.

Second Best Gear

Boundless Choreoe

Light Cone

Why?:

It provides a permanent CRIT Rate boost and can deal increased CRIT DMG on slowed and DEF-reduced enemies.

Effect:

Increase the wearer's CRIT Rate by 8% The wearer deals 24% more CRIT DMG to enemies that are currently DEF reduced or Slowed.

Band of Sizzling Thunder

Relic Set

Why?:

Sizzling Thunder is also good since it increases Acheron's Lightning DMG

Firmament Frontline: Glamoth

Ornament Set

Why?:

Firmament Frontline Glamoth's DMG buff can work well with Acheron as long as she has at least 135 SPD.

Tips for Acheron

Use In Simulated Universe

Acheron is a great character to use in Simulated Universe, as her Technique is able to kill basic enemies without needing to enter combat, saving time and reducing chance of dying, while still reaping the rewards.

Stock Up In "Trick Snack"

Acheron's Technique is excellent for skipping those boring and tidius fights all around the overworld, so make sure to stack up on "Trick Snack" as its the best consumable to keep refilling technique points.
